It was time for me to make a personal homepage, and a few hours exploring blogdown and the Hugo Academic theme later, here we are! At the time of writing this post, the page is still under construction, and more things will be added as we go. But it’s a start!

A few years ago, during my studies, I started the webpage Frisk og Funksjonell, where I together with some fellow students and colleagues published blog articles (in Norwegian) on different topics related to nutrition, health and science. That page will of course remain, as it’s a useful resource when these topics needs refreshing. Some of my articles will probably also appear in english here at some point.

Purpose

This page will be different. The main purpose of this page is to be a place to maintain an updated overview of my academic work, including my publications, conference presentations, my notes from courses and conferences, teaching material, my media appearances etc. You will also find an updated cv, and of course my contact information. In addition, I plan to create and maintain lists of resources I find useful, such as blogs/webpages, talks and papers. For now the page is in English, but parts of it might at some point be translated to Norwegian.

Blogging

I also plan to maintain a blog, with more or less regular posts on different topics, update on new pages etc.
